If you need help work your way down this list until you reach an acceptable solution:

* Check the [FAQ](/doc/#faq) and the [online documentation](http://github.com/activescaffold/active_scaffold/wiki)
* Search the [forum](http://groups.google.com/group/activescaffold) and the [issue tracker](https://github.com/activescaffold/active_scaffold/issues?sort=created&direction=desc&state=open) or [old issue tracker](http://code.google.com/p/activescaffold/issues/list) for information on your problem
* Use your favorite search engine to look for answers at other sites such as blogs and question-and-answer forums.
* If you feel your problem is due to a lack of understanding, post a question on the [forum](http://groups.google.com/group/activescaffold) being as specific as possible
* If you feel that you have a legitimate bug, post it on the [issue tracker](https://github.com/activescaffold/active_scaffold/issues?sort=created&direction=desc&state=open)

Please do not email any of the core team directly. Following these steps will help out everyone immensely so thank you.
